Shared Values. Shared Future.

Join a coalition that believes infrastructure should uplift communities, protect the planet, and serve the public good — not the status quo.

Ohlone Sky embodies the values of the United Nations’ 17 Sustainable Development Goals. Our gondola system is:

  • Cleaner than buses or cars, supporting climate resilience
  • More inclusive, connecting underserved communities
  • Smarter, using energy-efficient systems with minimal land disruption
  • Faster to build, and cheaper to operate, than traditional transit
  • Designed for longevity, with minimal environmental footprint

Let’s transform the Bay Area into a global model of sustainable urban transportation. Our coalition includes transit advocates, climate leaders, equity-focused nonprofits, designers, and citizens.
