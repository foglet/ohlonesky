Join the Coalition

The Ohlone Sky Coalition is a growing alliance of visionaries, advocates, and organizations committed to shaping a more connected, more sustainable Bay Area. We champion clean, swift, and equitable transit.

Our mission is to bring this future to life by building support among businesses, city leaders, regional partners, and state and federal agencies. Together, we can turn a bold idea into a lasting legacy.

This is a once-in-a-generation chance to reimagine how we move—not just across the Bay, but toward a more just and resilient future. Your support moves the project forward. Let's bring Ohlone Sky to life.
