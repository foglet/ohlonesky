Why Support Ohlone Sky?

Seven powerful reasons to join us in building a better, greener, more connected San Francisco Bay.

Climate Action, Now

Fully electric and emission-free, Ohlone Sky delivers zero tailpipe pollution and minimal energy use — helping San Francisco meet its climate goals without expanding roads or parking.

Equity in Motion

Ohlone Sky connects underserved neighborhoods like Treasure Island directly to downtown San Francisco. It supports — and strengthens — the Treasure Island Transportation Program’s vision: nearly every trip on and off the island can be made by transit, not by car.

Smarter Infrastructure

Faster to build and easier to permit than subways or light rail, Ohlone Sky uses less land, creates less disruption, and protects our waterfront and skyline.

Built for the Long Haul

Designed for a 100-year lifespan with durable systems and minimal maintenance needs, Ohlone Sky is a lasting public benefit — not a temporary fix.

A Global Model of Sustainability

Ohlone Sky brings the UN’s 17 Sustainable Development Goals to life — combining clean energy, inclusive access, and forward-thinking design to show what climate-conscious urban transit can be.

Tourism That Gives Back

With million-dollar views and effortless access to the Bay’s most iconic sights, Ohlone Sky is a visitor magnet — generating new revenue for San Francisco and Treasure Island while reducing car travel.

A Commitment to Giving

Ohlone Sky commits 10% of its annual revenue to charity — supporting local communities, climate initiatives, and cultural organizatioour ns. As a certified B Corp, we’re dedicated to using business as a force for good. We are transit with a conscience.

Join the Coalition

Ohlone Sky is more than a transit project — it’s a shared vision for a more just, livable, and sustainable Bay Area. We welcome partners from across sectors: climate leaders, transit advocates, equity-focused nonprofits, artists, designers, and citizens.
