Our Global Impact Starts Locally

Ohlone Sky isn’t just a ride from point A to B — it’s a commitment to people, the planet, and a regenerative transit future. We proudly support the United Nations’ 17 Sustainable Development Goals, especially those most relevant to life in San Francisco.

Climate Action

Zero tailpipe emissions. Less congestion. A cleaner Bay Area — one glide at a time.

Sustainable Cities & Communities

A sleek, modern transit solution that reduces our reliance on fossil fuel infrastructure.

Decent Work & Economic Growth

By linking San Francisco and Treasure Island, we unlock job access, tourism, and opportunity.

Reduced Inequalities

Our system connects underserved areas, closing the transportation gap for low-income residents.

Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Lightweight. Low-impact. Quick to build. Ohlone Sky sets a new standard for urban mobility.

Good Health & Well-being

Fewer cars mean cleaner air, less stress, and healthier communities across the Bay.

Life Below Water

By minimizing over-water construction and runoff, we help preserve the Bay’s marine ecosystems.
