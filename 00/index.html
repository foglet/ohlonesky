Ohlone Sky is a proposal for a gondola from Pier 24 to Yerba Buena and Treasure Island.

It lifts us above the noise, carrying us forward with grace, care, and conscience. Built for the City we love, and the future we owe — it’s transit as poetry, purpose, and promise.

Sustainable Development

We support solving challenges aligned with the United Nations’ 17 Sustainable Development Goals. Ohlone Sky advances goals around clean energy, resilient infrastructure, equitable mobility, climate action, and community uplift — not just for San Francisco, but for the world we want to live in.

Carbon Neutral

Fully electric and emission-free, Ohlone Sky helps San Francisco meet its climate goals without expanding roads or parking. With minimal energy use, a compact footprint, and near-silent operation, it eases congestion and lowers greenhouse gases as the island grows.

No Traffic

No cars. No sideshows. No congestion. Just smooth, reliable transit. For cyclists, it's a first-and-last-mile link across the Bay. For everyone, it’s freedom to move; climate-conscious urban transit. Twelve minutes end to end.

Connecting Communities

Operating 365 days a year, Ohlone Sky connects Treasure Island directly to the city’s core, generating tax revenue, jobs, and broader public benefit. Every trip to and from the island could be made by transit.

Extraordinary Views

Soaring above the Bay, you'll witness one of the most breathtaking urban panoramas on Earth. It’s not just transit — it’s a soaring journey. Like La Paz, London, Mexico City, New York, Paris (2025), and Portland — Ohlone Sky will become a new icon on the Bay — a floating thread that ties together our past, present, and future.

A Commitment to Giving

Ohlone Sky sets aside 10% of its annual revenue to nourish what moves us most: the communities we live in, the climate we share, and the cultures that shape us. As a certified B Corp, we see transit not just as a path from here to there, but as a thread that binds purpose to place. This is movement with meaning — designed to move society forward.
