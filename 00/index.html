Ohlone Sky is a proposal for a gondola from Pier 24 to Yerba Buena and Treasure Island.

Carry us forward. Bay to Sky.

No Traffic

No cars. No sideshows. No congestion. Just smooth, reliable transit gliding above the Bay—with poetry, purpose, and promise. For cyclists, it's the first-and-last-mile link across. For everyone, it’s freedom to move. Climate-conscious. Urban. Transit. Twelve minutes, end to end.

Carbon Neutral

Fully electric. Emission-free. Ohlone Sky helps San Francisco meet its climate goals—without widening roads or paving more parking. With minimal energy use, a compact footprint, and near-silent operation, it glides above congestion and lowers greenhouse gases as the island grows.

Connecting Communities

Operating every day, Ohlone Sky links Treasure Island directly to the heart of the city—generating tax revenue, creating jobs, and delivering public benefit. With each ride, a car-free trip to and from the island becomes not just possible, but preferable.

Extraordinary Views

As the City’s newest innovation, Ohlone Sky is not just transit—it’s a soaring journey. Cradled above the Bay, we move through light and air, as fog curls in from the Pacific and the water shimmers below. It frames one of the most breathtaking urban panoramas on Earth. And just like in La Paz and London, Mexico City and Portland, Ohlone Sky will become a new icon on the Bay—a silver thread weaving together our past, present, and future.

Sustainable Development

Ohlone Sky advances the city’s goals for clean energy, resilient infrastructure, equitable mobility, and climate action—while lifting communities and inspiring a model for cities everywhere. It’s not just for San Francisco, but for the world we want to live in.

A Commitment to Giving

Ohlone Sky sets aside 10% of its annual revenue to nourish what moves us most: the communities we live in, the climate we share, and the cultures that shape us. As a certified B Corp, we see transit not just as a path from here to there, but as a line of connection that binds purpose to place. As movement with meaning, Ohlone Sky advances society forward.
