Ohlone Sky is proposal for an aerial gondola spanning Pier 24 to Yerba Buena and Treasure Island in San Francisco.

It's more than a gondola — it’s a quiet revolution in the sky. It lifts us above the noise and haste, carrying us forward with grace, care, and conscience. Built for the City we love, and the future we owe — it’s transit as poetry, purpose, and promise.

Carbon Neutral

The gondola runs on electricity — zero tailpipe emissions. A win for the climate, and a breath of fresh air for our communities.

No Traffic

No cars. No congestion. Just smooth, reliable transit. For cyclists, it's a first-and-last-mile link across the Bay. For everyone, it’s freedom to move.

Connecting Communities

Operating 365 days a year, Ohlone Sky links Treasure Island, Yerba Buena, and the Embarcadero in just twelve minutes — bridging jobs, homes, and the waterfront.

Extraordinary Views

Soaring above the Bay, you'll witness one of the most breathtaking urban panoramas on Earth. It's not just transit — it’s a soaring journey.

A Commitment to Giving

Ohlone Sky commits 10% of its annual revenue to charity — supporting local communities, climate initiatives, and cultural organizations. As a certified B Corp, we’re dedicated to using business as a force for good. We are transit with a conscience.
