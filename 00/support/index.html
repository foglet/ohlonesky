Why Ohlone Sky?

Ohlone Sky isn’t just a fast, electric connection across the Bay — it’s a blueprint for a more just, sustainable, and inspiring city. Clean, quiet, and architecturally stunning, it links Pier 24, Yerba Buena Island, and Treasure Island.

It’s fast, fair, and future-ready.

A twelve-minute ride gives Treasure Island families the same access as the rest of San Francisco—no tolls, traffic, sideshows. Ohlone Sky closes the last-mile gap with fast, frequent, and affordable service. It’s not just a convenience—it’s a statement about the kind of city we want to be.

Climate-conscious by design.

Fully electric and emission-free, Ohlone Sky helps San Francisco meet its climate goals without expanding roads or parking. With minimal energy use, a compact footprint, and near-silent operation, it eases congestion and lowers greenhouse gases as the island grows.

Smarter, scalable infrastructure.

Ohlone Sky is faster to build and easier to permit than an extra bay tunnel, BRT or light rail. Gondola systems are modular—to boost capacity, just add cabins. With peak service every 20 seconds and throughput above 3,000 riders per hour per direction, it adapts to demand while complementing Muni, BART, and ferries.

Equity in motion.

Ohlone Sky connects underserved neighborhoods like Treasure Island directly to the city’s core. It aligns with the Treasure Island Transportation Program’s vision: every trip to and from the island could be made by transit, not by car. The result: expanded access to jobs, services, and opportunity.

Economic growth with purpose.

By connecting workers, visitors, and businesses, Ohlone Sky supports inclusive economic development. It strengthens Treasure Island’s commercial vitality and expands San Francisco’s transit-accessible labor pool — generating tax revenue, jobs, and broader public benefit.

Built to last.

Designed with a 100-year lifespan and easily updated, Ohlone Sky requires minimal maintenance yet delivers long-term reliability. It’s infrastructure that endures—not a stopgap solution.

It’s visually stunning and architecturally ambitious.

Like the gondolas of Medellín, London, or New York, Ohlone Sky will become a new icon on the Bay — a floating thread that ties together our past, present, and future. With a world-class design team ready to create a structure worthy of the skyline, it offers donors and sponsors the rare chance to embroider the visual identity and experience of San Francisco — forever.

Global values, local impact.

Ohlone Sky brings the UN’s 17 Sustainable Development Goals to life—combining clean energy, inclusive access, and forward-thinking design to set a global example for climate-conscious urban transit.

Tourism that gives back.

With million-dollar views and seamless access to the Bay’s most iconic sights, Ohlone Sky is a draw for visitors — generating new tourism revenue while reducing tail-pipe emissions.

A commitment to giving.

As a certified B Corp, Ohlone Sky donates 10% of its annual revenue to charity—supporting local communities, climate initiatives, and cultural organizations. Transit with a conscience.
