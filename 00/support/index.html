Join the Ohlone Sky Coalition

The Ohlone Sky Coalition is a growing alliance of visionaries, advocates, and organizations committed to shaping a more connected, more sustainable Bay. We champion clean, swift, and equitable transit—linking the waterfront, Yerba Buena, and Treasure Island by way of a soaring, zero-emissions aerial gondola.

Our mission is to bring this future to life—by building support among city leaders, regional partners, and state and federal agencies. Together, we can turn a bold idea into a lasting legacy.

This is a once-in-a-generation chance to reimagine how we move—not just across the Bay, but toward a more just and resilient future. We're seeking visionaries, donors, and community members who believe in sustainable, equitable transit. Your support moves the project forward.

The moment is here. Let’s bring Ohlone Sky to life.

A Commitment to Giving

Ohlone Sky sets aside 10% of its annual revenue to nourish what moves us most: the communities we live in, the climate we share, and the cultures that shape us. As a certified B Corp, we see transit not just as a path from here to there, but as a thread that binds purpose to place. This is movement with meaning—designed to move society forward.
