Join the Coalition To Advocate For This Project

What is it?

The Ohlone Sky Coalition is a diverse alliance of organizations advocating for better public transit to under-served areas, improved regional access to San Francisco’s job centers, reduced congestion, and cleaner air. We champion the study, development, and protection of potential aerial gondola sites connecting Yerba Buena and Treasure Islands to the city. Our goal is to win support from San Francisco, regional and congressional leaders, and state and federal agencies to approve and fund this transformative project.


Why join?

Today, 8,000 new homes are rising on Treasure Island, bringing 20,000+ new residents. Ferry service is expanding, but it won’t be enough.


An aerial gondola—fast, carbon-neutral, and dependable—offers a once-in-a-generation solution. It’s not just about transit:

  • It unlocks bike access from Yerba Buena Island to San Francisco.
  • It relieves future congestion before it becomes crisis.
  • It adds a striking, iconic new landmark to the Bay skyline.
  • And it welcomes the world, boosting tourism and connecting communities.

A vibrant Treasure Island makes for a stronger Bay Area.


This project isn’t just for today — it’s for generations to come.
