What is it

At the moment, 8,000 units of housing for 20,000+ people are being built on Treasure Island and the City is ramping up ferry service. Unfortunately, the population will quickly outpace current and future planned buses and ferries. There is one exception.


We propose a gondola (aerial cable car) between San Francisco and Treasure Island. It's a timely, efficient, effective and reliable carbon-neutral solution to access the island without driving and impacting Bay Bridge traffic. The benefits are considerable. It will provide vital transit infrastructure and become a major tourist draw and economic driver to the island.


Together a vibrant Treasure Island is a better Bay Area. Bay to Sky. Ohlone Sky.
