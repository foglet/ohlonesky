What is the Ohlone Sky Coalition?

The Ohlone Sky Coalition is a growing alliance of organizations committed to building a better future for the Bay Area.

We advocate for clean, fast, equitable transportation — connecting Treasure Island, Yerba Buena Island, and San Francisco through an iconic, carbon-free aerial gondola system.

Our mission is to earn the support of San Francisco leaders, regional and congressional representatives, and state and federal agencies — advancing Ohlone Sky from bold idea to living reality.

Coalition members agree to be listed on the website and in promotional materials.


Why join?

Treasure Island is transforming, with 8,000 new homes and over 20,000 new residents. Ferry service alone won’t meet tomorrow’s demand. The Bay Area needs another way — and it needs it now. Plus, the tourists.


Ohlone Sky will:

  • • Slash future congestion before it becomes gridlock.
  • • Connect bikes, ferries, trails, and neighborhoods.
  • • Run on clean electricity, with zero emissions.
  • • Offer breathtaking views and become a new civic icon.
  • • Boost tourism, economic opportunity, and community pride.

This is a once-in-a-generation opportunity to reimagine how we connect.

Not just for today — for generations to come.

Bay to Sky. Ohlone Sky.

The time to build is now. Join us.
