Ohlone Sky is proposal for an aerial gondola connecting Pier 24 to Yerba Buena and Treasure Island in San Francisco.


Carbon Neutral

The gondola system will run on electricity. Zero-emissions transit. — A win-win for climate and community.

No Traffic

It’s a boost for all public transportation. No cars on the road! For cyclists, this is the first and last mile across the Bay. Mobility for all.

Connecting Communities

Operating 365 days a year, a twelve-minute ride connects Treasure and Yerba Buena Islands to the Bay Trail, the Ferry Building and terminal, the downtown waterfront and beyond.

Extraordinary Views

You will experience unparalleled views across San Francisco Bay. It's visceral and about the journey.
