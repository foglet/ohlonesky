Ohlone Sky proposes a gondola from Pier 24 to Yerba Buena and Treasure Island.

Carry us forward. Bay to Sky.

No Traffic

No cars. No sideshows. No congestion. Just smooth, reliable, flowing above the Bay—with poetry, purpose, and promise. For cyclists, it's the first-and-last-mile link. For everyone, it’s freedom to move. Only twelve minutes.

Carbon Neutral

Ohlone Sky is fully electric and emission-free. With minimal energy use and a near-silent design, it helps San Francisco meet its climate goals while easing congestion. It supports resilient infrastructure, equitable mobility, and a livable future—not just for the Bay Area, but for the world we want to build.

Connecting Communities

Operating daily, Ohlone Sky links Yerba Buena and Treasure Island to the heart of San Francisco—generating tax revenue, creating jobs, and offering public benefit. Each ride makes a car-free trip simple and preferred.

Extraordinary Views

Ohlone Sky is a silver thread through time. We drifts above the Bay—between cloud and glinting tide—where fog spills from the Pacific and light catches steel and glass. What once clanged on hills now floats in silence. Past becomes present. The future rides with us, suspended in air.

A Commitment to Giving

Ohlone Sky dedicates 10% of its annual revenue to what moves us most: the communities we live in, the climate we share, and the cultures that shape us. As a certified B Corp, we see transit not just as travel, but as a thread that binds purpose to place. It moves us—physically, culturally, and collectively.
